Ann Brody is a social science researcher working toward briding the gap between academic fields and industry. Writing and speaking at the interscetions of anthropology, communication and science and technology studies, she is currently studying blockchain communities, attempting to understand how developers think about and relate to blockchain projects. When she is not thinking about open source and hackers, Ann is pondering on the subject of techno-thanotology and the future of cryogenic technology.
